About Me

Hi, I'm Lauren! I'm a current senior at UC Berkeley graduating with a degree in Cognitive Science this spring. I've always been intrigued by exploring how people think & perceive, which has sparked my curiosity in psychology, design, and education (and ultimately cogsci)! In addition to being a full-time student, I work as a web design assistant revamping the site for UC Berkeley's Center for the Build Environment , and as a research assistant running behavioral studies for the Whitney Lab for Perception & Action. As such, I'm excited to be learning more every day about how to design with humans in mind.

My friends know me as a lover of dogs, the Dodgers, and Dodger Dogs. You can catch me on the weekends drinking Vietnamese iced coffee and finding something new to learn (currently: reading this autobiography of Dr. Haing Ngor).
